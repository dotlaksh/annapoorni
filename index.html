Welcome to Annapoorni Grand !

Annapoorni restaurant was launched in the year 2003, became very popular and desired destination for many to enjoy multi cuisine delicacies. Thanks to our customers, they are our brand ambassadors rewarding us with growing popularity. Not only our customers grew in Bangalore but it was surprise and pleasure to see the increase in customers from neighbouring states. Lot of business travellers going out of city plan to get back to their destination with our takeaways, the only reason, customers enjoy the food we serve and want to carry the same to their families and friends. This is moment of pride for all of us at Annapoorni to see our reach widening

Annapoorni Restaurant offers a wide range of services in catering right from weddings to corporate events and other special occasions. We offer complete catering solutions for conferences, seminars, conventions and mega product launches. At Annapoorni we believe a menu is only as good as its execution. With this in mind we have set ourselves high Service standards. We use only the finest ingredients for our food; during the menu creation phase our chefs will recommend ingredients based on the season. 

Values and Vision

  • Quality Control Department monitored by Senior Management ensures a world class dining experience.
  • In house training of staff for exquisite service and quality of cuisine.
  • Hygiene is given top priority to be maintained to international standards.
  • Unique procurement system for selection of ingredients, those are handpicked and imported to maintain the genuineness of all recipes.
  • Consultant nutritionists cater to diabetic patients with special sugar free menu
